Steering Committee

Terms of Membership. After each WG, the Program Committee Chairs will join the WG Steering Committee for a period of three years. The default term of membership for all other Steering Committee members is five years. A new Chair of the Steering Committee will be chosen every five years as well. For continuity reasons, the current Chair of the WG Steering Committee may be on the committee for an additional year (after a new Chair has been chosen).
